# Suno (manuel) — Chicken Enchiladas cozy bake

## Style of Music

Warm acoustic-meets-lofi kitchen pop: brushed snare and soft wood-block taps like gentle prep rhythms, round nylon-plucked harmony stacks, muted electric bass pillow, tiny shaker grain panned wide. Verse whispers with close breathy doubles; pre-chorus lifts through stacked hums and doubled handclaps recorded dry; chorus blooms into glossy but intimate gang “ah” pads with a cinnamon-sparkle bell layer and subtle tape wobble. Include cozy oven-adjacent cues: soft metallic tick like cooling rack, brushed hi-hat like steam hiss, no aggressive drops. Clean mix, warm mid-focused, not cinema trailer huge.

## Exclude Styles

Heavy dubstep, trap brass, aggressive phonk, distorted 808 slides, drill hi-hats, orchestral epic trailer, reggaeton dembow loop clichés, mariachi pastiche, explicit artist mimicry, shouted hype vocals, ear-rattle sub drops, lo-fi crackle as the main hook, full Spanish folk ensemble parody, tutorial spoken steps.
