# Suno Prompt - TikTok Egg Boil

## Status

- Recipe: TikTok Egg Boil
- Goal: generate a long song that can be used as a full version, then edited into a shorter version for the vertical video.
- Target model: Suno 5.5 in Advanced / Custom Mode.
- Video use: TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels.

## Intent

A glossy, spicy pop prompt that mirrors the video arc: jammy yolk and red-gold sauce as the opening hook, shell crack and ice bath as tactile contrast, garlic-butter spice bloom as the build, and a final spoon lift with the Licorn character satisfied in the kitchen.

## Session notes (voice, length, hook)

Use these as operator notes before generating. They are not pasted into a single Suno text field as-is; align actions with Suno's UI.

**Voice**

- Select a bright playful character voice or vocal persona if available.
- Do not ask Suno to imitate a real singer, an existing artist, or any protected voice.

**Structure**

- Generate the long version first, aiming for 2 to 3 minutes.
- Make the chorus reusable for a 45 to 90 second vertical edit.
- Keep lines short, singable, repeated, spicy, glossy, and image-driven.

## Auto Lyrics Prompt

```text
Write original English song lyrics about a joyful unicorn food mascot making a spicy TikTok egg boil. Use only lyrics and section tags; do not explain the prompt. Do not include genre, production, instrument, mix, or vocal-style instructions. Do not write a recipe tutorial, quantities, numbered steps, brand names, software names, or social media references.

Mood: glossy, spicy, playful, seductive, satisfied, food-video ASMR energy, but not childish.

Lyric style: short singable lines, clipped phrasing, catchy repetitions, call-and-response energy, simple hooks, occasional parenthetical echoes like "(egg boil)". Keep the chorus very reusable for a 45-90 second video edit.

Story and imagery to weave in naturally:
- red-gold sauce flooding a jammy egg yolk
- shells cracking, ice clinking, smooth peeled eggs
- garlic butter, spice bloom, chili flakes, lemon spark
- glossy simmer, spoon ribbon, sticky sauce drip
- six eggs swimming in heat and shine
- parsley rain and a proud unicorn smile
- final bite: orange yolk melting into spicy butter

Must include naturally: TikTok egg boil, unicorn, garlic butter, chili glow, jammy yolk, red gold, spicy shine, lick the spoon.

Structure:
[Verse 1]
Open with jammy yolk, red-gold sauce, spicy shine, and the spoon.

[Pre-Chorus]
Build anticipation with crack, ice, peel, and heat waking up.

[Chorus]
Big chantable hook around TikTok egg boil + unicorn + red-gold spicy shine. Repeat "egg boil" as the hook.

[Verse 2]
Garlic butter, spice bloom, chili flakes, lemon spark, glossy simmer.

[Pre-Chorus]
The bowl fills up; sauce gets thicker and hotter.

[Chorus]
Repeat the same hook or a close variation.

[Bridge]
Softer sensual moment: yolk melts, sauce drips, parsley falls, the spoon comes close.

[Final Chorus]
Repeat with extra energy and a proud final image of the unicorn holding the red-gold bowl.
```
