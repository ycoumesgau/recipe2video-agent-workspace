# Suno — Arancini mozzarella, riz et petits pois

Copie manuelle dans Suno (pas d'API). Paroles par défaut en anglais ; pas d'imitation d'artistes identifiables.

## Style of Music

Italian-adjacent playful kitchen pop with airy acoustic guitar fingerpicks, pizzicato upright bass, brushed snare, and warm stacked hums; verses stay intimate with dry close-vocal breaths, pre-chorus lifts with tiny mandolin trills and shaker eggs, chorus blooms into neon-sweet analog synth pads that feel like golden fry glow and melted cheese shine; micro tape wobble for handmade warmth; tempo ~108 BPM; mix bright but soft transients to avoid fighting ASMR foley in the cut.

## Exclude Styles

Heavy dubstep; trap hi-hats; distorted 808 slides; lo-fi vinyl crackle loops; epic cinematic orchestra; looper tutorial trope; choir chant; reggaeton dembow; phonk; drill; vocal chops of famous singers; holiday bells; rap verses about recipes or measurements.
