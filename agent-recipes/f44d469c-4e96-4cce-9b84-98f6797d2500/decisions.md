# Decisions

## Source interpretation

- Source type: uploaded recipe photos.
- Recipe identified as "Arancini mozza, riz & petits pois" for 24 arancini.
- The final-dish photo is used as a visual guide for round golden crumb texture, rustic bowl service, young salad leaves, and the cut-open interior.
- The Thermomix-style written steps were translated into visible pan, countertop, and hand actions so the video reads as food ASMR rather than appliance operation.

## Editorial structure

- Opening hook: crisp cut-open arancino with steam, creamy rice, peas, and mozzarella. This avoids neutral mise en place and pays off the texture immediately.
- Logical scene count: 38 scenes, within the required 30-48 range.
- Seedance segment count: 7 segments, within the required 5-10 range.
- Context shots are limited to brief orientation beats; the storyboard favors macro detail shots for crust, rice, breading, oil, and cheese texture.

## Reference strategy

- Global Seedance references use the canonical names from the workspace asset reference system, including `@KitchenIslandDefault`, `@KitchenIslandOverhead`, `@InductionCloseup`, character references, and utensil references.
- Uploaded source photos are planned as `SourceFinalAranciniPhoto` and `SourceCutOpenAranciniPhoto` for serving composition and interior structure.
- Generated reference images are planned only for fragile food states: cooled pea risotto, sealed stuffed ball, double-breaded shell, fried golden crust, and cut-open mozzarella interior.
- No paid generation service was launched. Reference prompts are planning metadata only.

## Remaining gaps

- No clarifying questions are currently blocking the storyboard. If the production team wants exact character visibility in more than the final shot, that can be revised later without changing recipe physics.
